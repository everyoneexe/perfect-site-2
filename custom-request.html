Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our custom design process.

Our custom design process typically takes 2-4 weeks from concept approval to completion. This includes initial consultation, design development, client approval, and final production. Rush orders may be available for additional fees.

We accept JPG, PNG, WebP, PDF, AI, and PSD files up to 10MB each. For best results, please provide high-resolution images. If you have other file formats, please contact us directly.

We include up to 2 rounds of revisions in our custom design service. Additional changes may incur extra costs depending on the complexity. Major changes after production begins may require restarting the process.
